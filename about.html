Our Publishing Process

1

Manuscript Submission

Authors submit their completed manuscripts along with a query letter and synopsis. Our editorial team reviews all submissions within 7-14 days.

2

Editorial Review

Selected manuscripts undergo thorough editorial review including developmental editing, line editing, and proofreading to ensure publication readiness.

3

Design & Production

Professional cover design, interior formatting, and production for both digital and print formats. Authors are involved in all design decisions.

4

Global Distribution

Books are distributed across all major platforms including Amazon, Apple Books, Barnes & Noble, Kobo, and many more through our universal links.

5

Marketing & Promotion

Comprehensive marketing support including promotional materials, social media assets, and ongoing promotional campaigns to maximize reach.

6

Ongoing Support

Continued partnership with regular sales reports, marketing opportunities, and support for future projects. We're in it for the long term.
