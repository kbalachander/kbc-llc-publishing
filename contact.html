Frequently Asked Questions

What makes KBC LLC different from other publishers?

KBC LLC focuses on author partnership and global distribution. We offer personalized attention, comprehensive marketing support, and wide-reaching distribution through both traditional and digital platforms. Our authors benefit from Books2Read universal links, ensuring their books reach readers on all major platforms worldwide.

Do you accept first-time authors?

Absolutely! We welcome manuscripts from both established and debut authors. What matters most to us is the quality of your story and your commitment to the craft. We provide additional support and guidance for first-time authors throughout the publishing process.

What is your publishing timeline?

From manuscript acceptance to publication, the typical timeline is 3-6 months. This includes professional editing, cover design, formatting, and distribution setup. We work closely with authors to ensure quality while meeting agreed-upon deadlines.

Do you offer marketing support?

Yes, we provide comprehensive marketing support including book cover design, promotional materials, social media assets, and distribution to major retailers. We also help with book launch strategies and ongoing promotional efforts.

What rights do authors retain?

Authors retain full copyright ownership of their work. We negotiate publishing rights that benefit both parties, with terms clearly outlined in our publishing agreements. We believe in transparent, author-friendly contracts.

How do royalties work?

We offer competitive royalty rates that vary by format and sales channel. Authors receive detailed monthly reports and payments. Our goal is to ensure authors are fairly compensated for their creative work while maintaining sustainable publishing practices.
